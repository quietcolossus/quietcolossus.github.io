MyHealthMyRecord

Developed and led the evolution of MyHealthMyRecord, a video scrapbooking app designed to empower individuals with chronic conditions to document and analyze their daily experiences, offering actionable insights for both patients and healthcare providers.

Key Contributions

  • Native Development and Cross-Platform Expansion: Built the original Java-based Android app, later transitioning it to React Native for cross-platform compatibility with Android and iOS.
  • AI Integration: Integrated IBM Watson API for voice-to-text transcription and sentiment analysis, enabling patients to annotate their narratives effectively.
  • Machine Learning Innovation: Trained a TensorFlow neural network to recognize facial expressions in video recordings, enriching emotional context for patient and provider insights.
  • Data Visualization: Used D3.js to create dynamic visualizations, including word clouds and other interactive charts, to analyze and display patient-generated data.
  • Healthcare Insight Tool: Developed features to visualize patient narratives, providing healthcare providers with deeper insights into patient experiences between visits.
  • Leadership and Mentorship: Guided and trained new team members, fostering skills in mobile development, AI integration, and data visualization.

This project highlights expertise in healthcare technology, AI, and data visualization while demonstrating leadership in delivering innovative digital solutions.
